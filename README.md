Overview
Student Tank is a business analysis project developed as part of INFO 6215 - Business Analysis and Information Engineering at Northeastern University. It proposes a merit-based, AI-powered platform that connects student entrepreneurs with investors and mentors — eliminating barriers like geography, privilege, and lack of network access.

Problem Statement

60% of Gen Z wants to start a business, yet only 6% receive funding
Traditional funding channels (banks, VCs) are inaccessible to students with no credit history or connections
Student-led startups already generate $100M+ annually — imagine what they could do with proper support


Proposed Solution
Student Tank addresses this gap through:

AI-Driven Matchmaking — TensorFlow + BERT-based engine that pairs student startups with aligned investors based on 50+ data points
Video Pitch System — Replaces lengthy business plans with short, engaging video pitches
Tiered Mentorship — Personalized guidance matched to each startup's growth stage
University Verification — API-based student identity verification to build investor trust
Analytics Dashboard — Real-time tracking of pitch performance and investor engagement


Business Model

Revenue Streams: Freemium model, investor subscriptions, success fees, sponsorships
Key Partners: Universities, EdTech platforms, startup incubators, angel investors
Customer Segments: Student entrepreneurs, investors, universities


Research Highlights
Surveyed 150+ students across universities:

72% want to start a business while in school
61% cite funding as their biggest challenge
48% don't know where to find investors or mentors
87% said they would join a platform like Student Tank


Tech Stack

AI Engine: TensorFlow, BERT (NLP)
Video Platform: AWS MediaConvert
Security: AES-256 encryption, university API verification
Infrastructure: Docker, Kubernetes, New Relic monitoring
Data Integration: Crunchbase, PitchBook, CB Insights


Project Phases

Research & Planning — Problem validation, MVP definition
Design & Development — Prototype with AI matching and video pitch features
Testing & QA — Usability testing and bug fixes
Launch — University-level rollout and marketing
Continuous Improvement — Analytics-driven optimization
Expansion & Scaling — Global rollout to 200+ universities


Goals by 2026

Increase student startup funding by 300%
Reduce investor sourcing costs by 40%
Build a thriving global network of student entrepreneurs
